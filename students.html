To Perspective Students and Post-docs

  • Yes-Lab, led by Prof. Yanfang (Fanny) Ye at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU), is a team consisting of faculty, post-docs and students with research focuses of cybersecurity, data mining, machine learning and health intelligence. We very enjoy finding novel yet elegant solutions to resolve real-world problems that generate high impacts. Integrating humanity and technology, our long-term goal of research is to advance capabilities and trustworthiness of artificial intelligence (AI) to provide state-of-the-art innovations to secure cyberspace for its users and to improve the health and well-being of people around the world.
  • I am currently looking for Ph.D. students and Post-docs doing supervised research or independent study with me. If you are a well motivated and dedicated student pursuing a Ph.D. degree or post-doc related to the areas of cybersecurity, data mining, machine learning, and health intelligence, please send me an email with your CV.
